Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: At NextReach, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our services, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company details
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Profile Information: Business type, company size, preferences, profile picture
  • Communication Data: Messages, support tickets, feedback, and correspondence
  • Project Information: Business goals, target audience, content you provide for projects

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or use our services, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, navigation paths
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session data, preferences, analytics information

1.3 AI Assistant Interactions

When using our AI-powered tools:

  • Conversation history and queries
  • AI-generated responses and recommendations
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Feedback and ratings

1.4 Third-Party Data

We may receive information from:

  • Google Sign-In: Name, email, profile picture
  • Social Media Platforms: When managing your social media accounts
  • Analytics Providers: Aggregated usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Provide and personalize our services
  • Process your orders and payments
  • Communicate about your projects
  • Deliver customer support
  • Maintain and improve AI assistant accuracy

2.2 Business Operations

  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Improve our website and services
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property

2.3 Marketing and Communications

  • Send service updates and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Provide promotional offers and tips
  • Conduct surveys and gather feedback
  • Personalize marketing messages

2.4 AI Model Training

We may use anonymized conversation data to:

  • Improve AI response quality
  • Train and refine our models
  • Develop new features
  • Enhance user experience

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

NextReach does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal for payment processing
  • Email Services: For sending transactional and marketing emails
  • Cloud Hosting: For secure data storage and website hosting
  • Analytics Tools: Google Analytics for website performance
  • Communication Tools: For chat support and video calls

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Investigate fraud or security issues

3.4 Business Transfers

If NextReach is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure Storage: Encrypted databases and secure servers
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Authentication: Password hashing and multi-factor authentication options

4.2 Your Responsibility

Please help us protect your data by:

  • Using strong, unique passwords
  • Keeping your login credentials confidential
  • Logging out of shared devices
  • Reporting suspicious activity immediately

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide services
  • Required by law or for legal purposes
  • Necessary for legitimate business purposes

You can request account deletion at any time. Some information may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data

6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@nextreach.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6.3 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

6.4 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU residents have rights under GDPR, including:

  • Right to access, rectify, and erase data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority

7.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality

  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences

7.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (block all or specific cookies)
  • Our cookie consent banner
  • Privacy settings in your account
